Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Lucky Beach Bingo handles information when you use the game. Lucky Beach Bingo is designed to work without an account and does not ask you to provide personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Lucky Beach Bingo does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. The game does not require registration, a profile, or contact details.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

The game stores gameplay information locally on your device, including unlocked levels, star results, and music or sound preferences. This information is used only to provide game functionality and remember your settings. It is not sent to the developer.

4. Internet Access and Third Parties

The core game can be played offline. The current version does not include third-party advertising, analytics, social login, or cloud account services.

5. Children’s Privacy

Because the game does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe information has been provided to us outside the app, please contact us so we can address your request.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Gameplay progress and preferences remain on your device until you reset them in the game, clear the app’s local data, or remove the app. Uninstalling the app may permanently remove locally stored progress.

7. Security

We limit data handling by keeping gameplay progress locally on your device. No method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but the game does not operate a server-side account database for players.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the game’s features or legal requirements change. Any revised policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
