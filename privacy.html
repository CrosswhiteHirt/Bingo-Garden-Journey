This Privacy Policy explains how Bingo Garden Journey handles information when you use the game.

Bingo Garden Journey is designed as a casual game. The game may store gameplay progress, level status, stars, music settings, and sound settings locally on your device.

The game template may request permission for App Tracking Transparency and may use advertising SDKs to load and display ads. These third-party services may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate device information, ad interaction data, and diagnostic data according to their own policies.

2. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to save your game progress, remember your settings, display advertisements, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, improve stability, and support basic app functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may include third-party advertising, analytics, or security SDKs. These services may collect and process data independently. Their practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Children

The app is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review the request.

5. Data Storage

Gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Removing the app may delete locally stored progress.

6. Your Choices

You can control music and sound in the app settings. You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. You may also reset advertising identifiers and limit tracking through your device settings where available.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information related to the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy
